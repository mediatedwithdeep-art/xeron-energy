Who We Serve

Built for Solar for Every Scale —
Home to Factory

We serve homes, shops, factories and commercial buildings. Whether it's a 3kW rooftop for your home or a 500kW plant for your factory — we handle it all.

Manufacturing Units & Factories
High power draw, 24×7 load profiles, ideal for large rooftop or ground-mount
Warehouses & Logistics Parks
Large roof area, constant load — highest ROI potential with minimal civil work
Industrial Processing & Karkhaanas
High-temperature processes, heavy machinery — solar offsets peak demand charges
Commercial Complexes & Institutions
Hospitals, hotels, malls, educational campuses with 100+ kW load
Project Size: 3 kW (Home) – 1 MW (Factory)
We focus on projects where solar delivers meaningful savings on high electricity bills. The minimum viable project we undertake is 100 kW load capacity.
Electricity Bill Savings from Day 1
Our clients see 40–70% reduction in electricity bills. Our engineering team provides an honest payback projection before any commitment.
Full Compliance & Approvals Handled
DISCOM approvals, net-metering, structural load assessments, fire NOCs — we handle all DISCOM, subsidy and regulatory requirements end-to-end.
